Black Bean Enchiladas

Description

These black bean and rice enchiladas are so good, you won't care that they're vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 15oz. can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 14.5oz. can diced tomatoes and green chilies
  • 1/4 cup picante sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 8-10 gluten free tortillas
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup vegan shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 glass baking dish with a small amount of salsa.
  2. Cook green pepper and onion until tender in a skillet over medium heat, using small amounts of water to prevent sticking.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Stir in black beans, tomatoes, picante sauce, chili powder, and cumin. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered until mixture thickens.
  6. Stir in rice and most of vegan shredded cheese until cheese is melted.
  7. Fill each tortilla with about 1/2 cup of bean mixture. Fold in ends and roll up. Place enchiladas seam-down in baking dish.
  8. Pour remaining salsa and shredded cheese over all enchiladas.
  9. Bake 25 minutes.
