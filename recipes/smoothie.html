Fruit Smoothie

Description

A healthy breakfast or sweet treat for any part of the day. Use whatever fresh or frozen fruit you have on hand. If you like, make it vegan by using plant-based milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place frozen fruit, milk, and vanilla extract into a blender.
  2. Cover and blend until smooth.
  3. Marvel at the beautiful colors by serving in your prettiest glass cup.
