Bean Chili

Description

Use whatever beans you have on hand in this delicious meatless cold-weather meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 15oz. can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 10oz. can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • cooked rice, optional

Steps

  1. Cook green pepper and onion until tender in a skillet over medium heat, using small amounts of water to prevent sticking.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in chili powder, oregano, and cumin. Cook 1 minute.
  4. Stir in beans, tomatoes, and broth. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer about 20 minutes.
  6. If desired, serve over cooked rice.
